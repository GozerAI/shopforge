Features

Everything your commerce stack needs

Built for multi-channel operators who need clarity, not more dashboards.

SFY

Shopify integration

Pull products, orders, and inventory directly from your Shopify store via the Admin API.

INV

Inventory alerts

Get notified before you run out. Configurable low-stock thresholds across every connected storefront.

PRC

Pricing optimization

AI-driven pricing recommendations using cost-plus, competitor, and market strategies. Apply in one click.

MGN

Margin analysis

See gross margin and profitability by product, variant, and storefront. Spot what drains revenue instantly.

TRD

Trend enrichment

Products are automatically enriched with market trend signals, helping you prioritize what to push.

ORD

Order routing

Route Medusa orders to the right Shopify storefront based on niche, inventory, and fulfilment rules.

RPT

Executive reports

One-click reports for CRO, CFO, CMO, and COO. Share boardroom-ready summaries without a spreadsheet.

MDS

Medusa sync

Bi-directional product and order sync between your Medusa backend and Shopify storefronts, fully automated.
